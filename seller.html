Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to become a seller?

There's no upfront cost to become a seller. We take a small commission from each sale, which helps us maintain the platform.

What types of items can I sell?

You can sell pre-loved items in good condition, including clothing, accessories, home goods, electronics, and more. We prohibit certain items like weapons, counterfeit goods, and hazardous materials.

How do I get paid for my sales?

Payments are processed through your chosen payment method (bank transfer, PayPal, or Venmo) once the buyer confirms receipt of the item. Funds are typically released within 2-3 business days.

Who handles shipping?

Sellers are responsible for packaging and shipping items to buyers. You can set your shipping rates or offer free shipping to attract more buyers.

What happens if an item is damaged during shipping?

We recommend purchasing shipping insurance for high-value items. If an item arrives damaged, we have a resolution process to help sellers and buyers reach a fair solution.
